## Purpose

This form captures the compiled findings from a design review meeting. It organizes feedback by design area, assigns priorities, creates action items with owners and due dates, and provides an executive summary for decision-makers. This is the primary output of WI-DES-005 Step 5.7.

**Completed By**: Tech Lead (facilitator), immediately after design review meeting
**Distributed To**: All review attendees, CTO, PM
**Time Required**: 1-2 hours (post-meeting compilation)

---

## Instructions

1. **Complete within 24 hours** of the design review meeting while feedback is fresh
2. **Organize findings** from meeting notes into the categories below
3. **Assign priority** to each finding (P0 = Blocker through P3 = Minor)
4. **Create action items** with explicit owners and due dates
5. **Write executive summary** with overall assessment and recommendation
6. **Distribute to all attendees** and file in project documentation

**Important**: Every finding should result in either an action item or a documented decision to defer/accept.
