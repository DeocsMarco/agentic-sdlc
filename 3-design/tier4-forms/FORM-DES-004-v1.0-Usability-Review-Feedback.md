## Purpose

This form captures structured feedback during usability reviews of UI/UX designs. It standardizes feedback collection from internal reviewers, client stakeholders, and user testing sessions to ensure consistent evaluation and actionable outcomes.

**Completed By**: Reviewer (any stakeholder reviewing the designs)
**Collected By**: Designer
**Time Required**: 15-30 minutes per reviewer

---

## Instructions

1. **Review the prototype/mockups** before completing this form
2. **Walk through 2-3 primary user flows** in the prototype
3. **Score each category** on a 5-point scale
4. **Document specific issues** with location (screen/element) and severity
5. **Provide actionable suggestions** — say what should change, not just what's wrong
6. **Return form to designer** for compilation into review findings

**Important**: Focus on usability and user experience, not personal aesthetic preferences.
