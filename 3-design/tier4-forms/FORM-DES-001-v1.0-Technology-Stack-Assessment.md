## Purpose

This form captures a systematic evaluation of technology stack candidates for a project. It ensures technology decisions are based on objective criteria including team capability, project fit, ecosystem maturity, and total cost of ownership.

**Completed By**: Tech Lead, with input from CTO and Development Team
**Approver**: CTO
**Time Required**: 1-2 hours per layer (frontend, backend, database, infrastructure)

---

## Instructions

1. **Complete one form per technology layer** (e.g., one for Frontend Framework, one for Database)
2. **Evaluate 2-3 candidates** per layer using the scoring criteria
3. **Score each criterion** on a 5-point scale (1 = Poor, 5 = Excellent)
4. **Apply weight multipliers** to calculate weighted scores
5. **Document rationale** for the recommended selection
6. **Obtain CTO approval** before finalizing technology choice

**Important**: Technology choices impact the entire project lifecycle. Invest adequate time in evaluation.
