## Purpose

This form tracks the handoff of approved design documents and resources to the development team. It ensures every developer has the access, context, and materials needed to begin implementation. Complete this form during or immediately after the developer kickoff meeting (WI-DES-006 Step 6.6).

**Completed By**: Tech Lead and PM
**Time Required**: 30 minutes (during kickoff)

---

## Instructions

1. **Complete Section 1** (Team) by listing all development team members
2. **Verify access** for each team member against all required tools/systems
3. **Confirm document distribution** — every developer has received the design package
4. **Conduct kickoff meeting** and record attendance
5. **Collect developer acknowledgment** that they understand the design
6. **File completed form** in project documentation

**Important**: Do not consider handoff complete until all team members have confirmed access.
