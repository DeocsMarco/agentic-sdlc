## Purpose

This form captures a structured user persona profile for UI/UX design. Personas represent archetypal users based on research and requirements, guiding design decisions to serve real user needs. Complete one form per persona (target: 3-5 personas per project).

**Completed By**: Designer, with input from PM and Client
**Approver**: Design Lead
**Time Required**: 30-60 minutes per persona

---

## Instructions

1. **Base personas on real user data** from requirements gathering, stakeholder interviews, or user research
2. **Create 3-5 personas** representing primary user types for the system
3. **Mark one persona as "Primary"** — the most important user whose needs take priority in design conflicts
4. **Use personas actively** during wireframing and mockup phases to validate design decisions
5. **Review with client** to confirm personas match their understanding of users

**Important**: Personas are design tools, not documentation artifacts. They should be referenced throughout the design process.
