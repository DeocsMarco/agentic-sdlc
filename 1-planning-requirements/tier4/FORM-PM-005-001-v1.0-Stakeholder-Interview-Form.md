## Purpose

This form captures structured interview data from stakeholders to systematically gather functional requirements, non-functional requirements, constraints, and assumptions. It ensures complete requirement capture and reduces missing information by providing guided sections for all requirement categories.

**Completed By**: Project Manager or Business Analyst
**Approver**: Not required (information gathering form)

---

## Instructions

1. Schedule interview with stakeholder(s) for 60-90 minutes
2. Send this form to stakeholder 48 hours before interview as pre-read (optional)
3. Complete Section 1 (Stakeholder Information) before interview starts
4. During interview, complete Sections 2-7 by asking guiding questions and capturing responses
5. Within 24 hours of interview, finalize notes and send completed form to stakeholder for validation
6. Incorporate validated requirements into Requirements Document or User Story backlog

**Important**: Focus on capturing "what" stakeholders need, not "how" to build it. Technical solutions come later.
