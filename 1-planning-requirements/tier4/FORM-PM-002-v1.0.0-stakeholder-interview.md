## Purpose

This form captures stakeholder requirements systematically during interview sessions. It ensures complete requirement documentation across functional, non-functional, constraint, and assumption categories, supporting WI-PM-005 (Gathering Requirements and Creating User Stories).

**Completed By**: Project Manager / Business Analyst
**Approver**: Project Manager (for completeness review)

---

## Instructions

1. Schedule stakeholder interview and confirm attendance (minimum 60-90 minutes)
2. Review project objectives and scope with stakeholder before diving into requirements
3. Complete all sections during interview, capturing stakeholder's exact words where possible
4. For each requirement, assign a unique ID (REQ-###) for traceability
5. Prioritize requirements using MoSCoW method (Must/Should/Could/Won't)
6. Follow up on any unclear items within 24 hours of interview

**Important**: Document assumptions explicitly - these become critical for scope management and change control
