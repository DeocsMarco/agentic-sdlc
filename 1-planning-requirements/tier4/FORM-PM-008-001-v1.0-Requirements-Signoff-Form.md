## Purpose

This form captures formal client approval of project requirements, establishing the requirements baseline for scope control and change management. It provides a legal and audit-compliant record of what was agreed, when, and by whom, serving as the foundation for all subsequent project work.

**Completed By**: Project Manager
**Approver**: Client Decision Maker or Project Sponsor

---

## Instructions

1. Complete this form only after requirements documents are finalized and ready for client approval
2. Ensure all referenced documents (Requirements, User Stories, Objectives) have version numbers and are accessible to client
3. Schedule formal review meeting with client decision maker(s) to walk through requirements
4. Obtain signatures from all required approvers (typically client lead and project sponsor minimum)
5. Distribute signed form to project team, client, and file in project repository
6. Treat signed requirements as **baselined**—any changes after sign-off require formal change control process

**Important**: This form creates a contractual baseline. Do not rush client to sign if they have unresolved questions or concerns. Ensure full understanding and agreement before obtaining signatures.

### Section 4: Baseline Acknowledgment Statement

**By signing this Requirements Sign-off Form, the undersigned parties acknowledge and agree to the following:**

1. **Requirements Review**: We have reviewed all listed requirements documents and supporting materials in detail.

2. **Requirements Accuracy**: The documented requirements accurately reflect our needs, expectations, and business objectives for this project.

3. **Requirements Completeness**: To the best of our knowledge, all critical requirements have been captured. We understand that minor clarifications may emerge during design/development, but no major requirement categories are missing.

4. **Baseline Established**: The approved requirements documents constitute the **official requirements baseline** for this project. All project work will be planned, executed, and validated against this baseline.

5. **Change Control**: We understand that any changes to baselined requirements after this sign-off will be subject to formal change control processes, which may impact project timeline, budget, and resources.

6. **Scope Boundary**: We acknowledge the out-of-scope items listed in Section 3 are explicitly excluded from this project phase and will not be delivered unless added via formal change request.

7. **Deliverable Acceptance**: Final deliverables will be validated against these approved requirements. Deliverables meeting these requirements will be considered acceptable for client acceptance testing and sign-off.

## Legal & Audit Notes

This form serves as formal evidence of client approval for:
- **ISO 9001:2015 Clause 8.2.3**: Review of requirements related to products and services
- **Contract compliance**: Demonstrates client agreement to scope before work begins
- **Change management**: Provides baseline for evaluating change requests
- **Audit trail**: Documents who approved what and when

**Retention**: Retain signed copy for duration of project + 7 years (or per organizational retention policy).

**Amendments**: If requirements change after sign-off, create **Requirements Baseline v2.0** with new sign-off form referencing original baseline and changes made.
