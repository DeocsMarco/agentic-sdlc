## Purpose

This checklist ensures all prerequisites are verified before conducting a project kick-off meeting. It guides PMs through preparation steps to ensure the meeting runs smoothly and all participants have necessary information and materials.

**Use this checklist when:**
- Scheduled kick-off meeting is 2-5 business days away
- Contract has been signed and project has been authorized
- Initial project team has been assigned

**Time Required**: 30-45 minutes

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting this checklist, ensure you have:

- ☑ Signed contract and SOW (Statement of Work) available
- ☑ Project team members identified and assigned
- ☑ Access to project scheduling/collaboration tools
- ☑ Client primary contact information confirmed

---

## Checklist Items

### Section 1: Meeting Logistics

☐ **Item 1.1**: Confirm meeting date, time, and duration with all key stakeholders
  - **Expected Outcome**: Meeting scheduled on calendars of all required attendees (PM, client lead, tech lead, key stakeholders)
  - **Verification**: Calendar invitations sent and accepted by 80%+ of attendees
  - **Notes**: _[Document any scheduling conflicts or timezone considerations]_

☐ **Item 1.2**: Select and configure meeting platform (virtual or physical location)
  - **Expected Outcome**: Meeting room booked or virtual meeting link created and tested
  - **Verification**: Meeting location/link included in calendar invitation; virtual platform tested 24 hours prior
  - **Notes**: _[Record meeting platform, room number, or conferencing details]_

☐ **Item 1.3**: Identify and invite all required participants
  - **Expected Outcome**: Invitations sent to client decision makers, project team core members, and relevant stakeholders
  - **Verification**: Attendee list includes minimum: Client Lead, PM, Technical Lead, and 1+ client stakeholders
  - **Notes**: _[List key attendees and their roles]_

### Section 2: Agenda & Materials Preparation

☐ **Item 2.1**: Create meeting agenda using standard kick-off template
  - **Expected Outcome**: Agenda covers: Introductions, Project Overview, Objectives, Scope, Timeline, Roles, Communication Plan, Next Steps
  - **Verification**: Agenda includes time allocations for each section totaling meeting duration
  - **Notes**: _[Note any custom agenda items for this project]_

☐ **Item 2.2**: Prepare presentation materials (slides or handouts)
  - **Expected Outcome**: Visual materials created covering agenda topics with project-specific details
  - **Verification**: Presentation includes project name, objectives, timeline, deliverables, team structure
  - **Notes**: _[Document presentation format and storage location]_

☐ **Item 2.3**: Distribute agenda and pre-read materials to attendees 48 hours before meeting
  - **Expected Outcome**: All attendees receive agenda, contract summary, project overview, and any required pre-reading
  - **Verification**: Distribution email sent with all attachments and request for agenda review
  - **Notes**: _[Record distribution date and any attendee questions received]_

### Section 3: Project Documentation Review

☐ **Item 3.1**: Review signed contract and SOW for key details
  - **Expected Outcome**: PM has clear understanding of scope, deliverables, timeline, budget, and contractual obligations
  - **Verification**: Contract highlights documented including: total value, duration, key deliverables, acceptance criteria
  - **Notes**: _[Note any contract ambiguities or clarifications needed]_

☐ **Item 3.2**: Prepare initial project timeline (high-level phases)
  - **Expected Outcome**: Draft timeline showing major phases and milestones aligned with contract dates
  - **Verification**: Timeline includes start date, phase durations, key milestones, and delivery date from contract
  - **Notes**: _[Document timeline assumptions or dependencies]_

☐ **Item 3.3**: Document client objectives and success criteria from contract/initial discussions
  - **Expected Outcome**: Clear list of client objectives and how success will be measured
  - **Verification**: Objectives documented and ready to confirm with client during kick-off
  - **Notes**: _[Record any objective clarifications needed from client]_

### Section 4: Team & Resource Preparation

☐ **Item 4.1**: Confirm availability of assigned project team members for kick-off meeting
  - **Expected Outcome**: Core team members (Tech Lead, BA, QA Lead) confirmed attendance and prepared to introduce themselves
  - **Verification**: Team members briefed on project overview and their expected roles
  - **Notes**: _[List team members attending and any absences to address]_

☐ **Item 4.2**: Prepare team introduction materials (org chart or role descriptions)
  - **Expected Outcome**: Visual or written materials showing team structure, roles, and contact information
  - **Verification**: Team roster includes names, roles, contact info, and primary responsibilities
  - **Notes**: _[Note any staffing gaps or pending assignments]_

☐ **Item 4.3**: Set up initial project collaboration tools (repository, task board, shared drive)
  - **Expected Outcome**: Basic project workspace configured and ready for team use post-kick-off
  - **Verification**: Tools accessible to team members; initial folder structure or board columns created
  - **Notes**: _[Document tool URLs, access instructions, or pending setup items]_

### Section 5: Communication & Administrative Setup

☐ **Item 5.1**: Establish primary communication channels with client
  - **Expected Outcome**: Client contact details documented; communication preferences (email, Slack, MS Teams, phone) confirmed
  - **Verification**: Client primary and secondary contacts recorded with phone, email, and preferred method
  - **Notes**: _[Note client timezone, availability hours, or communication protocols]_

☐ **Item 5.2**: Prepare meeting notes template or recording setup
  - **Expected Outcome**: Note-taking method ready (template document, recording permission, designated note-taker)
  - **Verification**: FORM-PM-004-002 (Meeting Notes template) opened and ready; or recording consent process prepared
  - **Notes**: _[Specify note-taking approach and responsible person]_

☐ **Item 5.3**: Draft follow-up action item tracking method
  - **Expected Outcome**: System ready to capture and assign action items during meeting (task board, spreadsheet, or project tool)
  - **Verification**: Action item tracking location confirmed and accessible to all meeting participants
  - **Notes**: _[Document action item tracking tool and process]_

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Client stakeholders not responding to meeting invitation | Contact client lead directly via phone; request delegation if key stakeholder unavailable |
| Contract ambiguities discovered during review | Document specific questions and add to kick-off agenda for client clarification |
| Team members unavailable for kick-off meeting | Ensure at minimum PM + Tech Lead attend; schedule separate introduction for absent members |
| Virtual meeting platform issues during test | Have backup platform ready (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet); share backup link in advance |
| Pre-read materials not ready 48 hours before | Communicate delay to attendees; send materials ASAP with apology and request for review |
