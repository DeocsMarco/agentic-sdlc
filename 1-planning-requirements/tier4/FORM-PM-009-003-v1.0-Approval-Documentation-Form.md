## Purpose

This form captures formal leadership approval of project planning package, authorizing the project to proceed with execution. It documents approval details including authorized budget, approved resources, conditions/constraints, and serves as the official record of leadership go-ahead decision for audit and governance compliance.

**Completed By**: Project Manager
**Approver**: Leadership (VP, Director, or Sponsor with budget authority)

---

## Instructions

1. Complete this form immediately after receiving verbal approval in leadership review meeting (CHECK-PM-005)
2. Obtain signatures from all required approvers (typically primary sponsor minimum, sometimes multiple executives)
3. Distribute signed form to Finance, PMO, project team, and file in project repository
4. Update project status to "Approved - Execution Phase" in portfolio management system
5. Treat approved budget and resources as authorized baseline (changes require formal approval)

**Important**: This form authorizes project expenditures and resource commitments. Do not begin execution activities (hiring, purchasing, development) until this form is signed and approved.

## Legal & Audit Notes

This form serves as formal authorization for project expenditures and resource commitments. It provides audit evidence for:
- **Budget authorization**: Who approved what spending and when
- **Resource commitment**: Leadership approval of staffing plan
- **Scope baseline**: What leadership agreed to deliver
- **Risk acceptance**: Leadership acknowledged and accepted project risks
- **Governance**: Clear decision authority and reporting requirements

**Retention**: Retain signed copy for duration of project + 7 years (or per organizational retention policy).

**Amendments**: If approved budget, scope, or timeline changes significantly (>10%), create amended approval form referencing original and documenting changes.
