## 6. Intellectual Property

- All deliverables become Client property upon **full payment**.
- Provider retains rights to general tools, frameworks, and methodologies developed independently.
- Provider may use non-confidential project details in portfolio/case studies unless Client objects in writing.
- Pre-existing IP of either party remains with the original owner.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

- Both parties agree to keep confidential all non-public information shared during this engagement.
- Confidentiality obligations survive for **[2] years** after contract termination.
- Exceptions: information that is publicly available, independently developed, or required by law.

---

## 8. Change Request Process

- Changes to scope, timeline, or budget must be submitted in writing.
- Provider will assess impact and provide a Change Order within [5] business days.
- No change work begins until Change Order is signed by both parties.
- Approved changes may adjust timeline and/or fees.

## 10. Termination

- Either party may terminate with **[30] days** written notice.
- Upon termination:
  - Client pays for all work completed to date
  - Provider delivers all completed work and work-in-progress
  - Confidentiality obligations continue per Section 7
- Termination for cause (material breach): **[14] days** written notice with opportunity to cure.

---

## 11. Liability & Indemnification

- Provider's total liability shall not exceed the **total project fee**.
- Neither party is liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages.
- Each party indemnifies the other against third-party claims arising from their own negligence or breach.

## 12. Dispute Resolution

## 13. General Provisions

- **Force Majeure:** Neither party liable for delays due to events beyond reasonable control.
- **Entire Agreement:** This document supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.
- **Amendments:** Must be in writing and signed by both parties.
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remaining provisions.
- **Assignment:** Neither party may assign without written consent.
