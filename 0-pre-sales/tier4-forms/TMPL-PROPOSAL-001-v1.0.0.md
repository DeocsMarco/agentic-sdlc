## Purpose

This template provides the standardized structure for client proposals developed from approved project strategies. It ensures consistent, professional proposal presentation and includes all necessary sections for client decision-making and contract formation.

**Completed By**: Account Manager (AM) with C-Suite review
**Time Required**: 6-10 hours (depending on project complexity)

---

## Instructions

1. **Use approved PLAN-STRATEGY-001 as primary content source** for all sections
2. **Customize all placeholder text** with client-specific information
3. **Follow proposal preparation checklist** from WI-MGT-004 (embedded CHECKLIST-MGT-004-A)
4. **Maintain professional tone** throughout; proofread carefully for errors
5. **Obtain C-Suite review** before client submission
6. **Export to PDF** with professional formatting for client delivery

**Important**: This is a proposal template. All content must be tailored to specific client RFP requirements. Generic or boilerplate language should be minimized.

---

## Template Structure

This template contains 11 required sections that must appear in all proposals:

1. Cover Page
2. Executive Summary
3. Scope of Work
4. Deliverables
5. Timeline & Milestones
6. Pricing & Budget
7. Payment Terms
8. Acceptance Criteria
9. Assumptions
10. Terms & Conditions
11. Signature Page

**Purpose**: Standard contractual terms and legal protections

---

### Terms & Conditions

#### 10.1 Contract Formation

This proposal, upon acceptance by [Client Company Name], shall constitute a binding agreement between [Client Company Name] ("Client") and [Your Company Name] ("Vendor").

#### 10.2 Intellectual Property

- **Client IP**: All deliverables created specifically for Client under this agreement shall be the property of Client upon full payment
- **Vendor IP**: Pre-existing tools, frameworks, libraries, and methodologies remain property of Vendor
- **Third-Party IP**: Third-party software used is subject to its respective licenses
- **License Grant**: Upon full payment, Vendor grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use all deliverables

#### 10.3 Confidentiality

Both parties agree to:
- Maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information exchanged
- Use confidential information solely for project purposes
- Not disclose confidential information to third parties without prior written consent
- Return or destroy confidential information upon project completion

#### 10.4 Warranties

Vendor warrants that:
- Deliverables will conform to specifications in Section 3-4
- Work will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner
- Deliverables will not infringe third-party intellectual property rights
- Software will be free from critical defects for [90 days] after go-live (warranty period)

**Warranty Remedy**: During warranty period, Vendor will correct defects at no additional cost.

#### 10.5 Change Requests

Changes to scope, timeline, or budget require:
1. Written change request from Client
2. Impact assessment by Vendor (timeline, cost, risk)
3. Written approval by both parties before implementation
4. Amended contract if changes exceed [±10%] of original scope/budget

#### 10.6 Limitation of Liability

- **Cap**: Vendor's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total project cost
- **Exclusions**: Vendor is not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages
- **Exception**: No limitation for gross negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of confidentiality

#### 10.7 Termination

- **For Convenience**: Either party may terminate with [30] days written notice
- **For Cause**: Either party may terminate immediately for material breach if not cured within [15] days of notice
- **Payment Upon Termination**: Client pays for work completed and accepted through termination date

#### 10.8 Force Majeure

Neither party liable for delays due to events beyond reasonable control (natural disasters, war, pandemics, government actions).

#### 10.9 Dispute Resolution

- **Negotiation**: Parties will attempt good-faith negotiation for [30] days
- **Mediation**: If unresolved, parties will attempt mediation
- **Arbitration/Litigation**: If mediation fails, disputes resolved via [arbitration/litigation] in [jurisdiction]

#### 10.10 General Provisions

- **Governing Law**: This agreement governed by laws of [State/Country]
- **Entire Agreement**: This proposal constitutes entire agreement; supersedes prior agreements
- **Amendments**: Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties
- **Severability**: If any provision is invalid, remainder remains in effect
- **Assignment**: Neither party may assign without written consent
