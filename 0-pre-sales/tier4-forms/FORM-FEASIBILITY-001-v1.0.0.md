## Purpose

This form captures a systematic assessment of project feasibility across five critical dimensions: Technical, Resource, Timeline, Budget, and Strategic alignment. It supports the Go/No-Go decision for pursuing RFP opportunities.

**Completed By**: Account Manager (AM), with input from C-Suite
**Approver**: C-Suite
**Time Required**: 1-2 hours

---

## Instructions

1. **Complete all five feasibility dimensions** using the assessment criteria provided
2. **Score each dimension** on a 5-point scale (1 = Not Feasible, 5 = Highly Feasible)
3. **Document rationale** for each score with specific evidence
4. **Calculate overall feasibility score** (average of five dimensions)
5. **Make Go/No-Go recommendation** based on scoring rubric
6. **Obtain C-Suite review and approval** before proceeding

**Important**: A score of 2 or below in any critical dimension (Technical, Resource, Timeline) typically warrants a No-Go decision regardless of overall average.

### Dimension 1: Technical Feasibility

**Assessment Question**: Can we deliver the technical requirements with our current capabilities or reasonable skill acquisition?

**Scoring Criteria**:
- **5 - Highly Feasible**: Exact match with existing technical capabilities; have delivered similar projects successfully
- **4 - Feasible**: Strong match; minor skill gaps that can be closed quickly (< 2 weeks training/hiring)
- **3 - Moderately Feasible**: Partial match; some new technologies but within our domain expertise
- **2 - Challenging**: Significant skill gaps requiring major training/hiring (> 1 month); unfamiliar technology stack
- **1 - Not Feasible**: Requirements outside our technical domain; would require complete team replacement or partner

**Assessment Question**: Is the client's timeline realistic and achievable given project scope and our capacity?

## Go/No-Go Decision

### Decision Rubric

| Overall Score | Recommendation | Conditions |
|---------------|----------------|------------|
| 4.0 - 5.0 | **STRONG GO** | Pursue opportunity with confidence |
| 3.0 - 3.9 | **CONDITIONAL GO** | Pursue with risk mitigation plan; address low-scoring dimensions |
| 2.0 - 2.9 | **CONDITIONAL NO-GO** | High risk; only pursue if strategic imperative or gaps can be closed |
| 1.0 - 1.9 | **STRONG NO-GO** | Decline opportunity; not feasible |

**Special Rule**: Any individual dimension scoring ≤ 2 in Technical, Resource, or Timeline = **Automatic NO-GO** (regardless of overall average)
